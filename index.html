John Muir Trail - Day 04: Cathedral Lake

On day three of the JMT, we hiked from Sunrise Lake to Cathedral Lake. We spent the evening at Cathedral lake. On day four, we awoke to a sunlit lake, shown above. On day four of the JMT, we hiked from Cathedral lake to a campsite in Lyell Canyon.

A typical hiking day consists of:

  1. Breaking down the tent
  2. Having breakfast; usually oatmeal and coffee
  3. Hiking and snacking throughout the day
  4. Confirming out location on the map
  5. Finding and refilling out water
  6. Searching for a good campsite
  7. Setting up camp
  8. Cleaning off in a lake, river, or just with a washcloth
  9. Having dinner
  10. Journaling
